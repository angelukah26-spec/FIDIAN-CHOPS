01 · The Situation

Fidian Chops is already
running a serious operation.

The 3 locations in Jos. A full restaurant, a bakery, a catering arm, and a conference hall, all under one brand. The physical infrastructure for a major food business is already in place. What is missing is the digital layer that makes all of it visible, bookable, and orderable around the clock.

01
Two locations, one identity, but no digital home
POWA, state low cost and Old Airport Roundabout. Both operating. Both busy. Yet a customer who searches online for Fidian Chops finds nothing: no menu, no photos, no way to order. That customer goes elsewhere, and that revenue does not come back.
02
Four revenue streams, each capped by phone calls
Every order, every catering enquiry, every hall booking currently requires a phone call or a DM. That system has a hard ceiling, and it locks Fidian Chops out of the institutional buyers who will not book without a formal digital pathway.
03
The conference hall is Jos's best-kept secret
A high-revenue venue with genuine recurring potential: corporate meetings and so on. A line in a social media bio is not a sales channel. A professional booking page is.
04
The window to be first is open right now
Most food brands in Jos do not run an e-commerce website with online ordering. This is not a gap Fidian Chops is behind on, it is a market that has not moved yet. The first brand to build this will own it. That window will not stay open for long.
· Research Finding · Competitor Audit · Jos Food Sector ·

Zero restaurant, bakery, or food brands in Jos currently operate an e-commerce website with online ordering, a bookable conference venue, or a digital catering enquiry system. Fidian Chops is not behind. It is positioned to own a completely unoccupied space. First-mover advantage in local search, Google Maps rankings, and organic word-of-mouth will accrue to whoever builds first, and compound over time.

02 · The Problem

Three gaps costing
revenue right now.

Each of these is a daily leak. Together they represent the difference between where Fidian Chops is today and where it could be in 90 days.

01 🔍
Invisible on Google
Every search for "restaurant Jos", "birthday cake Jos", "conference hall Jos", or "catering Jos" sends potential customers to whoever has a website, not to Fidian Chops. It simply does not appear. That is not a marketing problem. It is an infrastructure problem, and it has a straightforward solution.
02 📲
Orders that cannot scale
Corporate clients, event planners, NGO procurement teams, and government departments do not send DMs to arrange catering for 150 guests or book a conference hall for a three-day training. They go to whoever has a professional digital presence with a form, a menu, and a structured pathway. Without that, the entire institutional market is inaccessible.
03 🏛️
A premium service with no platform
The conference hall represents recurring, high-ticket revenue: the kind that pays consistently and compounds as reputation grows. Right now it has no photos, no capacity information, no pricing guide, and no booking pathway online. Every week without a dedicated page is a week of missed enquiries from the exact clients who can afford to fill it regularly.

03 · The Anel's Solution

One platform. Every service.
Built to convert.

A WordPress e-commerce website engineered for everything Fidian Chops does: online ordering, a custom cake system, structured catering enquiries, conference hall bookings, and Paystack payments throughout. Not a template. A purpose-built digital operation.

Dedicated Homepage: Full Feature Build
Hero Section
Full-width banner with a strong Fidian Chops headline and three primary CTAs (Order Food, Order a Cake, Book the Hall) above the fold on every device. Visitors land and immediately know what to do next.
Order Now Gateway
Direct links from the homepage to the ordering system. One tap to get from landing page to checkout, with no navigation menus, no dead ends, no friction.
Featured Category Grid
Restaurant meals, pastries, catering packages, and conference hall. Four visual tiles, each one tap from browsing to ordering. Clean, fast, and immediately useful for every type of visitor.
Services Overview Strip
A concise visual summary of all four business lines with descriptions and individual CTAs. A corporate client looking for the hall and a customer looking for a birthday cake both land in the right place without searching.
Customer Testimonials
Real reviews displayed prominently for first-time visitors who need to trust before they buy. Social proof placed where it converts: early, and visually dominant.
Two-Location Cards
POWA, state low-cost and Old Airport Roundabout, each with address, hours, Google Maps link, and a direct WhatsApp contact button. No customer confusion about which branch to visit or call.
WhatsApp Floating Button
A fixed click-to-chat button on every single page of the website. Visitors on mobile (which is most of them) can start a conversation with Fidian Chops in one tap, from anywhere on the site.
Configurable Promo Banner
An updatable top banner for daily specials, seasonal promotions, event announcements, or limited offers. Editable by your team without touching a single line of code.

On-Page SEO + Google Analytics Structured for Jos search terms from day one: "restaurant Jos", "birthday cake Jos", "conference hall Jos". Tracked and measurable from launch.
